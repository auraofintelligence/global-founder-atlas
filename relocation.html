Soft-landing packages, startup and talent visas, digital e-residency and free-zone setups built to attract founders from abroad. Relocation is often optional, temporary or financially supported. A staged route that keeps you Minjerribah-based

Stay primarily in Australia, add an Estonia e-Residency EU company to unlock European grants, use Austrade Landing Pads for temporary market tests, and only base part of the year abroad (Abu Dhabi, Portugal, Singapore) where the package clearly justifies it. Most of these can begin before you incorporate anywhere.
