The plan

A 90-day funding search that respects your time.

Group compatible opportunities, build application materials once and reuse them, win the easy non-dilutive prizes first, then aim the big swings. The aim is treasure with proportionate paperwork, not chasing low-value programs.

How the score is built

Weighted ranking method

Every opportunity is scored 0 to 5 on eight criteria, then weighted into a single 0 to 100 score. Adjust the weights in the data if your priorities shift.
