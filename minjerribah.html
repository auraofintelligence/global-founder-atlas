Funding a pilot on Minjerribah.

International programs that could support an on-island pilot, monitoring project or small research facility on North Stradbroke Island / Minjerribah. These favour community-led, culturally appropriate work, so a local community or NGO vehicle and genuine Quandamooka partnership come first, before any application.

First things first

Any on-island initiative is offered as an invitation to partner, not a plan imposed on Country. Cultural authority, consent and benefit-sharing are settled with the community before a proposal is written. The programs below are matched for their fit with island biosecurity, environmental monitoring, climate adaptation and cultural storytelling.
