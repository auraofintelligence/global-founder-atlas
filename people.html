The people behind the funding

Money follows relationships.

Grants and visas are doors; communities, mentors and events are the people who open them. This page maps the networks worth belonging to, the home-ground allies, and the moments where founders, funders and collaborators are in the same room. These are communities and organisations, not private individuals: the right places to show up, contribute and be found.

How to use this page

Pick one community to join this quarter and one event to aim a trip at. Contribute before you ask: share work, help someone, show up consistently. For anything touching Minjerribah, start with the community, not the grant. Dates and membership cycles change, so confirm each at its official source.
